# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** January 1, 2026
**Last updated:** January 1, 2026

This privacy policy explains how jonathanduncan.pro ("this site", "I", "me") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit the site or engage with my services.

## What I Collect

### Information You Provide

When you contact me through the contact form, email, or booking link, I collect:

- Your name and email address
- Any message content you send
- Scheduling information when you book a call via Cal.com

### Information Collected Automatically

When you browse this site, I may collect:

- **Analytics data:** Pages visited, time on site, referral source, and general geographic region. This data is collected through Google Analytics and is used solely to understand how visitors use the site and improve the experience.
- **Cookies:** This site uses a small number of cookies for analytics and to remember your cookie consent preference. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

## How I Use Your Information

- To respond to your enquiries and schedule consultations
- To understand how the site is used and improve its content
- To send project-related communications if you become a client

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Cookies

This site uses cookies only for:

- **Analytics cookies** (Google Analytics): to measure site traffic and usage patterns
- **Consent cookie:** to remember whether you accepted or declined analytics cookies

You can accept or decline analytics cookies using the banner shown on your first visit. You can also clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

## Data Retention

- **Analytics data** is retained in Google Analytics according to their standard retention settings.
- **Contact form messages and emails** are retained for as long as needed to respond to your enquiry or support an active client relationship.
- **Scheduling data** is managed by Cal.com according to their retention policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** any personal data I hold about you
- **Request correction** of inaccurate data
- **Request deletion** of your data
- **Withdraw consent** for analytics cookies at any time by clearing your browser cookies

To exercise any of these rights, email me at [jonathan@jonathanduncan.pro](mailto:jonathan@jonathanduncan.pro).

## Security

I take reasonable measures to protect your information. This site is served over HTTPS. Client project data is handled under separate confidentiality agreements.
