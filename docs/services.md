# Services

Every engagement starts with the same question: **What is the real problem?**

I don't sell hours or feature lists. I solve business problems with custom-built software, and every service below follows the same model: understand the business constraint first, then design the right technical solution.

!!! info "Most engagements combine 2–3 of these services."

    A typical path: discovery sprint → MVP or pilot → full platform build → ongoing partnership. The discovery call figures out the right sequence for your situation.
